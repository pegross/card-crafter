§ 01

Philosophy & the frozen contract

The three Phase-1 commitments stay inviolable: deterministic (fixed per-hour rates, no rolls — only which forage type you find is random; edibility is authored per plant), traceable (every insult, incubation dose, and stage carries a cause-stamp to the obituary; nothing kills without a named condition), and legible (the panel is the 6 permanent bars + however many condition bars are active right now — every driver is a real bar you can watch, never a hidden chip).

The council's four drafts contradicted each other on numbers, so the synthesis froze one source of truth. This is the table a builder implements:

§ 02

The gauges — three tiers

Tier A · permanent sliders (always visible)

Calories

Fuel, drain 4.5/hr. Up: eating. Down: exertion surcharge + gut malabsorption. <20 halves sleep quality; <10 jolts you awake.

Hydration

Water, drain 5.0/hr — the signature meter of gut illness & fever. The axis you must out-drink to survive illness (mult ≤1.8, suspended asleep).

Warmth special

Body temp — driven only by the ambient lerp, never the mult table. Exertion pushes it up; Wet steepens cold loss. Any mult:{Warmth} is a silent no-op — conditions touch it additively.

Energy

Same-day stamina, drain 4.0/hr — signature of fatigue. Hard-capped at 100 − 0.6×fatigue. →0 = "sheer exhaustion" death.

Immune

Resistance, tiny 0.2/hr base. <40 → gut illness recovers ×0.6 and enters faster — the master illness-progression modulator (watched, reversible).

Mental ≤2 drivers

Steadiness. Down from fatigue (primary) + gut illness at Dysentery only (the "sickness makes you sad" edge). Low Mental → worse sleep → more fatigue → the watched spiral.

Tier B · condition bars (appear when active, below the six needs)

Gut Illness → Hydration

Waterborne, per-stage decay 1.2/0.9/0.6. Dysentery lingers 2–3 in-game days. Fed by matured incubation doses; drives Fever at ≥Loose Stool; slowed by low Immune.

Hypothermia own lethal

Rises only when Warmth-low AND Wet both hold — rate ~(35−Warmth)×(1+Wet/100). Stage-3 "Deep" fires its own collapse stamped "the cold" — never mislabeled exhaustion.

Tier C · driven bars & hidden inputs

Fatigue own bar → Energy cap

Sleep-debt, the multi-day arc. Shows as its own bar (rising = more debt) and also caps Energy at 100−0.6×fatigue (a notch on the Energy bar). Only sleep clears it.

Wet input bar

Rain/wading/sweat up; fire/indoors dries. Steepens the Warmth cold-loss line and is the AND-half that spawns Hypothermia. A bar while you're wet, gone when dry.

Fever driven

No direct cause — driven by gut illness ≥Loose Stool. Hits Hydration (sweating) + Energy. Fever→Warmth is cosmetic only, so you can never get sick to stay warm or mask the cold.

Incubation invisible

A queue of pending doses {id, amt, ready_min, cause}. A dawn unboiled drink hatches into gut illness by dusk — full dose dumped instantly, the drink-time cause preserved. No chip by design.

§ 03

The influence web

One new ordered _apply_influences() pass, reading last-tick values (_prev) so it's deterministic and can't feed-back explode. The load-bearing edges:

  • Action exertion → Calories− (burn) · Hydration− (thirst) · Energy− · Warmth+ (metabolic heat) · fatigue+. One scalar makes "activity → calories/thirst/body-temp" all true at once.
  • Rain / wading / sweat → Wet+ ; fire or indoors → Wet− (dries).
  • Warmth-low AND Wet → Hypothermia+ (AND-gate). Stage-3 → own lethal collapse, "the cold".
  • Gut illness → Hydration− (capped 1.8, suspended asleep) · Mental− at Dysentery only · Fever+ at ≥Loose Stool.
  • Fever → Hydration− (sweating) + Energy−. Warmth effect cosmetic only.
  • Fatigue → Energy ceiling (100−0.6×fatigue) · Mental− (primary) · Exhausted → Immune−. Only sleep clears it.
  • Low Mental / cold / sick nights → sleep_quality− (clear less debt) — the fairness lever: hold the basics and you recover.
  • Immune <40 → gut recovery ×0.6 & faster enter; Hydration>60 & rested → gut decay ×1.4. The master resistance modulator.
§ 04

Rest · Sleep · Fatigue

Three distinct layers make Rest non-substitutable for Sleep. Energy is gas in the tank now. Fatigue (hidden) is sleep-debt, the multi-day arc. The load-bearing rule: energy_cap = 100 − 0.6 × fatigue — high sleep-debt means you literally cannot be energetic, not with food, not with rest, because only sleep lowers fatigue and thus raises the ceiling.

Rest (rewritten)Energy +0 (removed entirely — the whole point). Exertion 0.4 so needs barely drain, Mental +3 (capped 60, diminishing on repeats), and no illness-decay bonus (moved to sleep-only, so Rest can't spam-heal a gut bug). Rest steadies your nerves; it can never touch fatigue or the ceiling — so rest-spam only delays and worsens.
Sleep (the only true reset)All recovery moves inside advance_time(mins, exertion, sleeping): clear fatigue first (14/hr × quality), then raise Energy toward the freshly-risen ceiling. (This deletes the current _sleep() bug where modify("Energy",15) ran before advance_time and clamped to the pre-sleep low ceiling — which is exactly why sleep felt weak.) You wake naturally at fatigue 0, or jolt awake if a critical meter crosses <10 — "You wake shivering, the fire dead": a ruined night, never an unforeseeable death.

sleep_quality = clamp(0.35 + 0.45×Warmth/100 + 0.20×Mental/100, 0.3, 1.0), hard-capped at 0.5 if Calories or Hydration <20. Cold/hungry/despairing nights clear as little as ~5/hr — the deterministic, traceable spiral if you can't hold the basics.

Exploit table (why nothing is free)rest-spam → 0 Energy / 0 fatigue relief, self-defeating · eat-your-way-out → food clamps to the ceiling, never lowers fatigue · nap-spam → every chunk drains all meters + burns fuel + a bad-condition sleep clears almost nothing · oversleep-to-heal → forced wake at fatigue 0, extra sleep is pure loss. No action both passes time cheaply and restores — the only real recovery is a prepared, vulnerable sleep.
§ 05

Illness, made to linger

Illness must be a real cost you accept only when necessary — the opposite of today's 8/hr clear-in-an-hour. Four mechanisms, all dict-only + one small queue:

  • Per-stage decay replaces the flat 8/hr. A single unboiled sip (→Queasy) clears in a few hours (fair for the common case); Dysentery takes 2–3 days on its own — brutal, so you treat it. recovery_mod bends it: ×1.4 when hydrated & rested, ×0.6 when Immune <40.
  • Delayed onset via the pending-dose queue — a drink enqueues {amt, ready_min = now + 4–8h, cause}; maturation dumps the full dose and copies the drink-time cause verbatim, so a Day-5 death still reads "Dysentery (unboiled water, Day 3 06:10)."
  • Fairness cap (the "unwinnable Dysentery" fix): composed mult capped at 1.8 (Dysentery bites ~9/hr awake — out-drinkable with banked boiled water), and the illness Hydration drain is suspended while prone, so a water-banked night is always survivable.
  • Med economy as fixed constants: Broth −8 (scarce salt), Charcoal −30 (finite), Antibiotics −50 (finite, severe). A hard cap of one dose / stage / day with steep diminishing returns stops broth-spam. Boiled water doesn't cure — it only refills what the bug drains, burning your clean-water/fuel stock.

Deterministic forage poisoning: only which type you find is random; edibility is authored (wood-sorrel edible, lords-leaves tox 35, death-cap tox 95 = a fixed lethal mistake, never bad luck). Eating unknown offers Eat-blind (full nutrition + full toxin, IDs it), Cautious taste-test (fractional both, IDs — the smart hedge), or Field guide (safe ID). Knowledge is permanent per playthrough — accrued, never rolled.

§ 06

UI — one widget, calm by default

No chips — every gauge is a real bar. The six needs are always-visible bars; each condition (gut illness, fatigue, hypothermia, fever, wet…) renders as its own bar in a conditions section directly below them, appearing when it becomes active and vanishing when it returns to zero. The bar's fill is its severity, tinted by how bad it is, with a small rising/steady/easing tick beside it. Panel = 6 need-bars + however many condition-bars are active; healthy = just the six. The bars are the simulation: you watch a rising illness bar bend a falling Hydration bar in real time.

Fatigue gets its own bar (sleep-debt) and notches the Energy bar at its ceiling (100−0.6×fatigue), so you see both the debt climbing and the lid it puts on Energy. Live legibility: a per-need hover tooltip decomposes the current drain into base + each condition's named contribution this tick, so a falling need-bar always maps to the exact condition-bars pulling it down. Plus a per-tick "biggest Mental mover: X" log line so even a multi-source despair death names its cause.

§ 08

Open questions — your calls

  1. Med economy constants. How many charcoal/antibiotic doses per run, how scarce is salt? "Long-lasting illness" only means something once these sit between consequence-free and unwinnable.
  2. Is the 1.8 multiplier cap right? It must make Dysentery out-drinkable awake yet still threatening — needs tuning against real clean-water/fuel throughput once the boil loop is timed.
  3. Weather dependency. Wet/Hypothermia need a rain/exposure source that doesn't exist (outdoor_temp is static, no rain). Phase 2d needs at least a minimal weather driver — where does it live?
  4. Malnutrition needs a diet-monotony tracker (foods eaten over N days) — deferred, but forage-ID in 2e should probably start recording variety to feed it.
  5. Fatigue→Mental strength: is a stage mult on the tiny 0.3/hr base enough to feel, or does it need an additive term? A 2-line playtest branch.
  6. Obituary aggregation: is the per-tick "biggest Mental mover" line enough, or should the obituary aggregate multiple simultaneous contributors (it currently names one)?
  7. Jolt-awake survivability: does waking from a ruined night leave enough fatigue-clearance to fix the emergency, or can a Dysentery night + dead fire still checkmate a prepared player? Needs a concrete survivability trace.
  8. Exertion calibration: one scalar drives fatigue, Calories/Hydration surcharge, and Warmth heat at once — are 0.4/1.0/1.6 balanced across all three, or do they need decoupling?
