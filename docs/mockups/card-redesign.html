What changed

  • Image area ~132px tall (was 44) — the card is now image-forward, so dropped-in art has real space. Placeholder shows a faint monogram + an "image" marker.
  • Card grew to 172 × 290 from 140 × 214 — more presence, and the description still gets 2–3 comfortable lines under the art.
  • Tighter area margins: the section padding, the gap between cards, and the row insets all shrank, so bigger cards don't cost the layout its breathing room.
  • Kept: area headers (On the Ground, Out There · Lordly Manor), the category tag, terse titles, and the state bar with its %.
  • Accent stripe on the top edge already encodes the category by color (cold = place, amber = station/fixture, grey = item/resource, green = you-are-here) — reinforcing the tag.
